About Me

Multipotentialite author and creator passionate about storytelling, technology, and creativity

Arshad Anis Ahmed Shaikh is a multipotentialite author and creator passionate about storytelling, technology, and creativity. With a background in writing, web development, and audio production, he brings a unique blend of skills to everything he creates.

His debut work, System Has Crashed, explores the crossroads of modern life, emotion, and identity. When he's not writing or recording, Arshad can be found reading, nurturing garden vibes, or curating thoughtful content for his growing audience.
